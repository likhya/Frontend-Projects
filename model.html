Short Paragraph on Butterfly.

Butterflies are considered as one of the most beautiful and fascinating creatures on Earth. Butterflies and adult moths are harmless, but their caterpillars are often severe pests for plants, plant products and specific products of animal origin. Millions of dollars are spent each year to fight harmful caterpillars.
